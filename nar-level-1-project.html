High Power Rocket - NAR Level 1 Certification

A high-power rocketry project focused on stability correction, airframe fabrication, recovery system integration, launch, and successful vehicle recovery.

High-Power Rocketry Aerodynamic Stability Recovery Systems Fabrication NAR Level 1
Role Rocket design, fabrication, launch prep
Focus Stability and recovery
Outcome Successful NAR Level 1 certification

Project Overview

This project centered on designing, building, launching, and recovering a high-power rocket for National Association of Rocketry Level 1 certification. The work emphasized safe launch preparation, stable flight, and reliable recovery.

Stability & Flight Readiness

The vehicle required attention to center of gravity, center of pressure, static margin, and mass distribution before flight. Stability corrections were validated before launch to support a safe certification attempt.

Build & Recovery System

  • Assembled the airframe, motor mount, and recovery hardware.
  • Integrated parachute recovery components for safe descent.
  • Prepared the vehicle for launch inspection and certification flight.
  • Recovered the rocket intact after launch.

Outcome

The rocket launched successfully and was recovered intact, validating the stability and recovery approach used for the certification flight.
